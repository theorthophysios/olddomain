Our Glorious Leader

Michael graduated from The University of Queensland, Bachelor of Physiotherapy programme in 1995. After consolidating his skills in Australian Hospitals he moved to the UK to further his experience in Private Practice, including time spent at the Oxford Nuffield Orthopaedic Hospital. Michael returned to Australia and commenced his career in Orthopaedic Physiotherapy in 2002. Since then, among other things Michael has been Chair of the Queensland Orthopaedic Physiotherapy Network for many times, completed his PhD on knee surgery and raised a small family.
