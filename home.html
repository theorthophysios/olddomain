About

The OrthoPhysios offer injury management, surgery preparation and rehabilitation Physiotherapy. Preparation for orthopaedic surgery and recovery from surgery is our focus. Knee, hip, ankle, upperlimb and spinal surgery rehabilitatioin is delivered in a way that meets your needs. We pride ourselves in our close communication with your GP and Specialist Doctor. This way your health care team is connected and working towards the best outcomes for you.
